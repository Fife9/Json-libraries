More About the Photographer

Alex Fife is an IDM student at the Tandon School of Engineering in Brooklyn, NYC. He finds love in taking pictures and listening to new musics. Some of his inspirations in art are the surrealist artist Man Ray, the musician Lou Reed, and the writer Joseph Campbell.

In his photography, he likes to focus on stagnant objects and landscapes mostly. His camera of choice is a Canon EOS 5D. Currently available for hire, he is seeking employment as photographer for zines of any kind. A zine with a focus on the music industry would be his dream job.
